About Her

Hi there! I'm the baker behind Crumb of Her Own — a home sourdough enthusiast who believes there's nothing better than a warm loaf fresh from the oven. From tangy country loaves to fluffy English muffins, I love experimenting with flavors, fermentation, and flour. This little corner of the internet is where I'll share my recipes, my process, and all the happy accidents along the way. Thanks for being here — we're so excited to see you!
